About Us

The Roar Kingdom is your ultimate gateway to the mesmerizing world of wildlife. We are dedicated to providing immersive eco-tourism experiences in India’s most breathtaking national parks and wildlife sanctuaries.

Our tailored tours and stays ensure you connect with the wonders of the wild, while promoting conservation and sustainability.

Our Tours

Dive into the wilderness with our meticulously crafted tours, combining thrilling adventures, sustainable travel, and unmatched hospitality. Each journey is designed to immerse you in India's natural beauty while prioritizing conservation and safety.

Ranthambore Jungle Safari

Embark on a guided safari in Ranthambore National Park to witness Bengal tigers, leopards, and a vibrant array of wildlife.

  • Duration: 6 hours
  • Starting from: ₹3,500
  • Available: Year-round

Features include eco-friendly transport, expert guides, and unparalleled safety measures.

Kaziranga Bird Watching

Explore the avian wonders of Kaziranga National Park with an expert guide to spot exotic bird species and learn about their habitats.

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Starting from: ₹2,500
  • Available: October - March

Enjoy tailored routes, expert-guided tours, and breathtaking photographic opportunities.
